Whether you’re carrying the weight of trauma, navigating stress, rebuilding after loss, or simply trying to find your footing again, Randy offers a place where you can be heard, understood, and supported.

Who I help

Randy works with adults, first responders, veterans, and individuals in high‑stress or high‑impact roles. He supports people navigating trauma, anxiety, depression, substance use, grief, and major life transitions. Whether someone is carrying long‑term emotional weight or facing a new challenge, Randy provides a grounded, compassionate space to work through it.

What I Offer

Randy provides evidence‑based therapy tailored to each person’s needs. His work includes trauma‑informed care, EMDR, CBT, DBT, and support for anxiety, depression, substance use, and life transitions. He also specializes in working with first responders, veterans, and individuals in high‑stress roles.
